Functional medicine is built around seeing the full picture. Rather than treating symptoms in isolation, functional medicine clinicians look at the patient’s genetics, environment, lifestyle, nutrition, history, and underlying causes of dysfunction. The Institute for Functional Medicine describes functional medicine as a framework for identifying and addressing the underlying processes causing imbalance and disease in each individual.
