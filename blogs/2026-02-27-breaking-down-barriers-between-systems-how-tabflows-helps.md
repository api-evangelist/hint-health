Running an independent membership-based practice is rewarding, but can also be shockingly overwhelming. Every day, staff and clinicians move between multiple systems: EMRs, billing platforms, messaging tools, and membership management software like Hint. Each of these systems plays an important role in delivering excellent care, but switching back and forth creates friction that adds up to lost time and unnecessary frustration.
